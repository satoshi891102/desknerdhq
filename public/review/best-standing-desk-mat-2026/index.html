Standing Without a Mat Is Almost as Bad as Sitting

The whole point of a standing desk is better health. But standing on a hard floor for hours creates its own problems: foot pain, knee strain, lower back fatigue. An anti-fatigue mat provides a cushioned surface that encourages subtle weight-shifting and reduces joint stress.

We tested 5 standing desk mats over 3 months, with each tester standing for 4+ hours daily. We measured time-to-discomfort (how long before foot fatigue sets in), material durability, and edge stability (do you trip on curled edges?).
