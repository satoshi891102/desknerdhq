The Eye-Level Rule

The top of your monitor should be at or slightly below eye level. For most people, that means raising the monitor 4-8 inches above the desk surface. A monitor riser solves this while often adding storage space underneath ‚Äî a two-for-one ergonomic and organizational upgrade.

We tested 6 monitor risers across three categories: basic platforms, shelf systems, and adjustable risers. Each was evaluated for height appropriateness, stability with a 27" monitor, build quality, and the bonus: how much useful storage they create underneath.
