Best Ergonomic Mice 2026: 6 Tested to End Wrist Pain

Your Mouse Is Hurting Your Wrist

A standard mouse forces your forearm into pronation ‚Äî palm-down ‚Äî for 8+ hours daily. This position compresses the carpal tunnel and strains the forearm muscles. Ergonomic mice address this by changing the grip angle, and the difference in comfort after switching is dramatic.

We tested 6 ergonomic mice across three design philosophies: vertical (handshake grip), trackball (stationary body, moving ball), and sculpted (traditional shape, better angles). Each was used for two weeks of full-time office work.

2. Logitech ERGO M575 ‚Äî Best Trackball

If you want to eliminate arm movement entirely, a trackball is the ultimate ergonomic solution. The M575 stays stationary while you control the cursor with your thumb on the ball. Zero forearm movement means zero forearm strain.

The learning curve is steeper ‚Äî expect a week before you feel proficient. But every tester who stuck with it reported the most comfortable mousing experience of the group. At $50, it's also excellent value.
