Qi vs. Qi2 vs. MagSafe: What Matters

  • Qi (standard): 5-10W for most phones. No magnetic alignment ‚Äî you place the phone manually. Works with any Qi phone.
  • Qi2: Up to 15W with magnetic alignment. The open standard version of MagSafe. Works with iPhone 12+ and newer Android phones.
  • MagSafe: Apple's proprietary 15W standard. Functionally identical to Qi2 for iPhones. MagSafe chargers also work as Qi2 chargers.
