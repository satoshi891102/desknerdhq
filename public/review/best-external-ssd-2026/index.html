Best External SSDs for Home Office 2026: 5 Tested for Speed and Reliability

By DeskNerdHQ Team Updated 2026-02-14 13 min read

Why You Need Local Backup

Cloud storage is great until your internet goes down, your provider has an outage, or you need to transfer a 50GB video file to a colleague standing next to you. A portable SSD provides instant, reliable access to your files at speeds that make cloud storage feel like dial-up.

We tested 5 portable SSDs over 3 months, measuring real-world transfer speeds (not marketing claims), temperature under load, and reliability across 1,000+ plug/unplug cycles.
