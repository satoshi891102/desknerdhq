Power Strips: Boring But Critical

Nobody gets excited about a power strip. But if you're running a home office with a monitor, laptop, desk lamp, phone charger, and maybe a printer, you need one. And the difference between a good power strip and a bad one is the difference between a clean desk and a tangled mess of adapters.

We tested 5 desk power strips over 3 months, focusing on three things: surge protection (are your $2,000 worth of electronics actually protected?), USB-C Power Delivery (can it charge your laptop directly?), and design (does it look like it belongs on a nice desk?).
